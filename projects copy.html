Respondind and Recovering from a disaster

Responding to Disaster

The aftermath of a disaster can be overwhelming. Here's a guide to immediate actions: Check for injuries and attend to first aid needs. Contact emergency services if necessary. Listen to official updates and follow instructions. Stay calm and help others in need


Learn More
Recovering from Disaster

Disaster recovery is a long-term process. Here are some steps to help you move forward: Assess the damage to your home and property. Contact your insurance company. Seek emotional support and connect with your community. Utilize available resources and government assistance programs.
