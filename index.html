What We Do

Disasters can disrupt our lives in an instant. Whether it's a natural phenomenon like a hurricane or earthquake, or a man-made event like a technological failure or hazardous materials spill, being prepared can significantly minimize the impact on you, your family, and your community.
This website is your one-stop resource for all things disaster management. Here, you'll find everything you need to: Understand your risks: Learn about the common natural and man-made hazards that threaten your area. Develop a plan: Create a comprehensive emergency plan for your household, business, or school. Build your kit: Assemble an emergency kit stocked with essential supplies to sustain you during a disaster. Stay informed: Get real-time emergency alerts, warnings, and evacuation information.
